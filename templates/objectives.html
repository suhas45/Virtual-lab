OBJECTIVES

The Database Management System (DBMS) Virtual Lab aims to provide an interactive environment for students to explore fundamental concepts and practices in managing databases. The lab objectives include:

  1. Understanding the principles and functionalities of database management systems
  2. Practicing database design, implementation, and querying techniques
  3. Exploring advanced topics such as transaction management, concurrency control, and security in databases
